Other Movies

I Want To Eat Your Pancreas

In this deeply moving first-person story, an introverted high school boy finds his classmate’s diary―and learns her biggest secret. Yamauchi Sakura is dying from a pancreatic disease and now he is the only one person outside her family to know the truth. The last thing the boy wants is to be her friend, but Sakura’s cheerful demeanor and their shared secret draw them together in this heartrending tale of friendship and mortality.

Hello World

Produced by Graphinica and distributed by Toho, the film is set in a futuristic Kyoto where a high school student named Naomi Katagaki encounters a person claiming to be himself, who time-traveled from 10 years in the future to save a classmate named Ruri Ichigyō.

A Silent Voice

A coming-of-age story in which a tween boy grows into adolescence trying to make amends for the hurt he caused a deaf girl due to his merciless bullying.

Garden of Words

The Garden of Words is considered a romance and drama film. It opens at the start of the rainy season in Tokyo with Takao Akizuki ( Akizuki Takao?), a 15-year-old student and aspiring shoemaker, opting to skip his first class and sketch shoe designs in the garden at Shinjuku Gyoen.

5 Centimeter Per Second

a 2007 anime drama-romance in which two tweens who experience first love slowly drift apart as the years go by. It's a tearjerker told in three chapters, each covering a different era in the lives of the main character.

In The Forest of Fireflies

A six-year-old girl named Hotaru Takegawa gets lost in a forest and is found by a mask-wearing spirit named Gin. Gin tells Hotaru that he will disappear if touched by a human, but they become friends and she visits him in the forest. As Hotaru grows up, she finds it difficult to maintain their friendship, but they eventually go on a date at a festival organized by spirits.

Your Name

Taki, a boy from Tokyo, and Mitsuha, a girl from a small mountain town, wake up in each other's bodies for weeks at a time. They initially think it's a dream, but eventually realize it's real and start leaving notes for each other. They help each other become more like themselves, and begin to develop feelings for each other. However, they discover that something is wrong when they try to find each other as themselves.

Weathering With You

Hodaka Morishima runs away from his troubled home to Tokyo, where he meets Hina Amano, an orphaned girl who can control the weather. Hodaka befriends Hina and helps her market her "Sunshine Girl" skills, but the more she uses her powers, the more it becomes clear that there's a personal cost.

Tokyo Godfathers

he film follows the story of Gin, a middle-aged alcoholic, Hana, a transgender woman, and Miyuki, a teenage runaway. The three discover the baby in a garbage dump and decide to return her to her parents. Along the way, they confront their own pasts and learn to face the future together.
